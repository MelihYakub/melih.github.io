About

Hi, I'm 19 year old boy from Bulgaria. Currently I am a university student and I study Software technology and design. I speak 5 languages (English, German, Turkish, Bulgarian and Azerbaijani). I started designing with basic mobile Photoshop apps, but it didn't took me long until I learn Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects.

Turning point of my design life was my internship at turkish sports chanel Hastalik Bu Futbol. There is where I was forced to learn to use more profetional apps. I also worked with lots of pro football players like Valeri Bojinov, Ilias Hassani, Spas Delev and Ivan Kokonov.
